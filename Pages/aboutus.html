About Us

Chiven Classic is a luxury fashion brand that's all about timeless elegance, sophistication, and empowering individuals to express their unique style. Founded by Chidiebere Emenike and co-founded by Chinaecherem Emenike, we're passionate about delivering exceptional products and services that exceed our clients' expectations.

Our Story

Chiven Classic was born out of a passion for fashion and a desire to create pieces that inspire confidence and beauty. Our founder, Chidiebere Emenike, has spent years honing her craft, and her vision for Chiven Classic is to make luxury fashion accessible and inclusive.

What We Do

At Chiven Classic, we design, create, and deliver stunning pieces that blend traditional Nigerian flair with modern sophistication. From bespoke tailoring to ready-to-wear collections, our products are crafted with precision, attention to detail, and a commitment to quality.
